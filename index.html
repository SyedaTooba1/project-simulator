SIMULATION

This is a queuing simulator designed to assist users in understanding and analyzing different queuing models. It supports M/M/1, M/M/c, M/G/1, M/G/c, G/G/1, and G/G/c models. This tool is perfect for students and professionals eager to learn about queuing theory concepts.

This simulator models and analyzes queuing systems, supporting configurations such as M/M/1, M/M/c, M/G/1, M/G/c, G/G/1, and G/G/c. It allows users to input data and generates detailed customer information, which is useful for both single-server and multi-server setups.
